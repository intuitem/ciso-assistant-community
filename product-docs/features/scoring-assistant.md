# Scoring Assistant

Trouble assessing your risk?

Based on the [OWASP Risk Rating Methodology](https://owasp.org/www-community/OWASP_Risk_Rating_Methodology), the scoring assistant helps you determine the risk level for your scenario. Choose between **technical** or **business** impact and answer the questions.
