# Perimeters

> _Draft — placeholder._

A **perimeter** is the concrete scope an assessment or risk study applies to, inside a domain. Where a domain defines _who_ owns the work and _what_ they can see, a perimeter defines _exactly what is being assessed_ — a product, a system, a process, a contract.

- Why perimeters exist as a separate object instead of putting everything on the audit.
- How multiple audits and risk assessments can share a perimeter.
