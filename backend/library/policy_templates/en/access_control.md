# Access Control Policy

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes requirements for controlling access to the organization's information systems, applications, and data. It ensures that access is granted based on business need and least privilege principles.

## 2. Scope

This policy covers all access to organizational information systems, networks, applications, and data, regardless of location or method of access.

## 3. Access Control Principles

- **Least Privilege**: Users shall be granted the minimum level of access required to perform their duties
- **Need-to-Know**: Access to information shall be restricted to individuals who require it for their role
- **Separation of Duties**: Critical functions shall be divided among different individuals to reduce risk of fraud or error

## 4. User Account Management

### 4.1 Account Creation
- Accounts shall be created only upon authorized request from the user's manager
- Each user shall have a unique identifier
- Generic or shared accounts are prohibited except where technically necessary and approved

### 4.2 Account Modification
- Access rights shall be modified when a user's role changes
- Managers are responsible for requesting access changes for their team members

### 4.3 Account Termination
- Access shall be revoked immediately upon termination of employment
- Access shall be reviewed and adjusted upon transfer to a different role
- Dormant accounts shall be disabled after 90 days of inactivity

## 5. Authentication

### 5.1 Password Requirements
- Passwords shall meet minimum complexity requirements
- Passwords shall be changed at regular intervals
- Password reuse shall be restricted
- Default passwords shall be changed immediately upon deployment

### 5.2 Multi-Factor Authentication
- MFA shall be required for remote access
- MFA shall be required for privileged accounts
- MFA shall be required for access to sensitive systems and data

## 6. Privileged Access

- Privileged accounts shall be inventoried and assigned to named individuals
- Privileged access shall be used only when necessary and for the minimum duration required
- All privileged actions shall be logged and monitored
- Privileged credentials shall be stored in an approved password vault

## 7. Remote Access

- Remote access shall be provided through approved secure channels only (VPN, zero-trust)
- Users shall not use personal devices for remote access unless enrolled in the mobile device management program
- Remote sessions shall be terminated after a defined period of inactivity

## 8. Access Reviews

- User access rights shall be reviewed at least quarterly for sensitive systems
- All access rights shall be reviewed at least annually
- Reviews shall be documented and any discrepancies corrected promptly

## 9. Physical Access

- Physical access to data centers and server rooms shall be restricted to authorized personnel
- Visitor access shall be logged and escorted
- Physical access rights shall be reviewed regularly

## 10. Policy Review

This policy shall be reviewed at least annually and updated as needed. Changes must be approved by the Information Security Officer.
