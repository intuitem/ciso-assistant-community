# Information Security Policy

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the framework for managing information security across the organization. It defines the principles, responsibilities, and requirements for protecting information assets from threats, whether internal or external, deliberate or accidental.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All employees, contractors, and third parties with access to organizational information
- All information assets, including data, systems, networks, and physical infrastructure
- All locations where organizational information is processed, stored, or transmitted

## 3. Information Security Objectives

The organization commits to:
- **Confidentiality**: Ensuring information is accessible only to authorized individuals
- **Integrity**: Safeguarding the accuracy and completeness of information and processing methods
- **Availability**: Ensuring authorized users have access to information when required

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities

### 4.1 Senior Management
- Approve and champion the information security policy
- Allocate resources for information security initiatives
- Review security performance and risk posture regularly

### 4.2 Information Security Officer
- Develop and maintain security policies and procedures
- Coordinate security awareness and training programs
- Monitor and report on security incidents and compliance

### 4.3 All Employees
- Comply with information security policies and procedures
- Report security incidents and vulnerabilities promptly
- Complete required security awareness training

## 5. Risk Management

The organization shall:
- Conduct regular information security risk assessments
- Implement controls proportionate to identified risks
- Maintain a risk register and review it periodically
- Accept, mitigate, transfer, or avoid risks based on the risk appetite

## 6. Asset Management

- All information assets shall be identified and classified
- Asset owners shall be assigned and documented
- Handling procedures shall align with the classification level

## 7. Access Control

- Access to information shall be granted on a need-to-know basis
- User access rights shall be reviewed regularly
- Strong authentication mechanisms shall be implemented
- Privileged access shall be restricted and monitored

## 8. Incident Management

- A security incident response plan shall be maintained
- All security incidents shall be reported and investigated
- Lessons learned shall be incorporated into improved controls
- Relevant authorities shall be notified as required by law

## 9. Compliance

- The organization shall comply with applicable legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements
- Regular audits shall be conducted to verify compliance
- Non-compliance shall be addressed through corrective actions

## 10. Policy Review

This policy shall be reviewed at least annually or when significant changes occur. All amendments must be approved by senior management.
